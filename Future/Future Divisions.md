# Future Divisions

[[NestShield]] is built to absorb new divisions without restructuring. Each plugs into the existing [[Career Framework]] and [[Revenue Funnel]].

---

## Planned / Potential Divisions

- Fire & Life Safety
- HVAC Monitoring
- Energy Monitoring
- Indoor Air Quality
- Security Integration
- Construction Consulting
- Capital Planning
- Facility Management
- Insurance Risk Services
- AI & Building Analytics

## How New Divisions Plug In

See [[Scalability]].

Each new division:
1. Defines positions within the existing 7 [[Career Classes]]
2. Maps them to [[Technical Track]] or [[Leadership Track]]
3. Adds division-specific certifications to [[Promotion Rules]]
4. Adds to [[Division Ladders]]
5. Optionally adds division-specific rows to the [[Competency Matrix]]

The class system doesn't change. The framework absorbs new divisions — it doesn't bend for them.
