# Scalability

The [[Career Framework]] and [[Revenue Funnel]] are designed to work whether [[NestShield]] has 5 employees or 500.

---

## What Scales

- **Career system:** 7 [[Career Classes]] + 2 tracks ([[Technical Track]], [[Leadership Track]]) absorb any new division. See [[Future Divisions]].
- **Funnel:** New service lines can become new layers or sub-layers without restructuring the existing 4.
- **Competency standards:** The [[Competency Matrix]] applies company-wide. Division-specific rows are additive.
- **Promotion process:** [[Promotion Rules]] are universal. Division-specific certs are the only variable.

## What Doesn't Change

- The 7 career classes
- The dual-track structure
- The principle that every funnel layer is profitable standalone
- The rule that deliverables surface the next problem
- [[Documentation Standards]]

## What Flexes

- Number of divisions
- Number of positions per division
- Division-specific certifications
- Industry verticals served
- Geographic coverage
