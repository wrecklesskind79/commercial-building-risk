# Layer 2 — Plan

**Service:** [[Preconstruction and Estimating]]
**Division:** [[Commercial Building Risk]]
**Revenue:** Flat fee or percentage of estimated cost

---

## Entry Triggers

- Handoff from [[Layer 1 — Assess]] (inspection found work needing budgets)
- Direct: contractor needs bid support, owner needs a project budget
- Referral: architect or PM sends plans
- Repeat client

## Step-by-Step SOP

### 1. Define Scope (30 min call)

- What's the project? (Renovation, repair, new construction, TI, capital plan)
- What documents exist? (Plans, specs, inspection reports, prior estimates)
- Detail level needed? (Conceptual, detailed, bid-ready)
- Timeline driver? (Financing, board approval, construction start, insurance)
- Who uses this estimate? (Owner budgeting, contractor bidding, lender underwriting)

### 2. Collect Documents (within 48 hours)

- Plans and specifications
- Your [[Layer 1 — Assess|inspection report]] if this came from Layer 1
- Geotech, environmental, structural reports
- Owner's budget expectations
- Local cost data

### 3. Build the Estimate

- Quantity takeoffs from plans or field measurements
- Price with current market data
- Include GC, OH, contingency appropriate to estimate level
- Break down by CSI division or building system
- **Flag assumptions explicitly**
- **Flag scope gaps** — things missing from plans that will cost money

### 4. Write Deliverable

- Cover letter: scope, basis, key assumptions
- Summary by division/system (the page the decision-maker reads)
- Detailed line items
- Exclusions and qualifications
- **Risk flags** — the handoff section

### 5. Deliver and Review

Walk through it. Never just email.
- Start with the number
- Explain major cost drivers
- Discuss risk flags and contingency
- Discuss phasing if total exceeds budget

## Handoff to Next Layer

See [[Handoff Scripts#Layer 2 to Layer 3]].

**Triggers:** Recurring costs signaling systemic problems, water-related findings, systems nearing end of life, insurance loss patterns, client says "we keep spending money on this."

**If no handoff needed:** Invoice, collect, ask to bid the next project.
