# Networking SOP

**Stage:** Pre-funnel
**Purpose:** Generate first clients for the [[Revenue Funnel]]

---

The business is pre-revenue. Every interaction is top-of-funnel right now.

## Where to Go

Go where building owners are:
- Commercial real estate events
- Property management associations (BOMA, IREM local chapters)
- Insurance industry events
- Contractor associations
- Construction industry meetups
- Local CRE networking groups

## What to Say

Lead with the problem, not the product.

> "I help building owners figure out where they're losing money on their buildings."

NOT: "I sell water leak monitoring."

## The One Question

> "What's the biggest headache with your buildings right now?"

Then shut up and listen. Their answer tells you which [[Revenue Funnel]] layer they enter.

## Follow-Up

- **Within 24 hours.** Email, not LinkedIn message.
- Reference something specific from the conversation.
- Offer value first:

> "I'd be happy to walk your building and give you a quick read on what I see — no charge for the conversation."

This is NOT a free inspection. It's a 30-minute site visit that qualifies whether a paid [[Layer 1 — Assess|inspection]] makes sense.
