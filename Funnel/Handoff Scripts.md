# Handoff Scripts

Exact language to transition clients between [[Revenue Funnel]] layers. These aren't sales pitches — they're observations from the work you just did.

---

## Layer 1 to Layer 2

> **From [[Layer 1 — Assess|Inspection]] → [[Layer 2 — Plan|Estimating]]**

> "We found $X in deferred maintenance across these systems. Some of this needs to be addressed in Year 1. You're going to need real numbers to plan this — not the ranges I put in the report. I can put together detailed estimates so you know exactly what you're looking at. Want me to scope that out?"

**Use when:** Multiple deferred maintenance items, capital planning needs, renovation scope, bid packages needed.

---

## Layer 2 to Layer 3

> **From [[Layer 2 — Plan|Estimating]] → [[Layer 3 — Advise|Risk Consulting]]**

> "There are a few things in here that aren't just construction costs — they're ongoing risk. The water supply system, the roof drainage, the lack of freeze protection on these exposed lines. You can fix them once, but without a plan to monitor and maintain, you'll be back here in three years. I do risk consulting for exactly this — want me to put together an assessment of your ongoing exposure?"

**Use when:** Recurring maintenance costs signal systemic issues, water-related findings, systems nearing end of life, insurance loss patterns, client says "we keep spending money on this."

---

## Layer 3 to Layer 4

> **From [[Layer 3 — Advise|Risk Consulting]] → [[Layer 4 — Protect|NestShield]]**

> "We can fix the maintenance gaps and that handles the known problems. But the unknown ones — the pipe that fails at 2 AM on a holiday weekend, the freeze event nobody catches until the ceiling falls in — those need continuous monitoring. That's what NestShield does. It watches the building 24/7 and shuts the water off before the loss happens. Based on your loss history, one prevented event pays for the system. Want me to spec it out for your building?"

**Use when:** Water risk exposure, freeze exposure, water loss history, no existing leak detection, insurance pressure, high-value contents.

---

## Layer 1 Direct to Layer 4

> **From [[Layer 1 — Assess|Inspection]] → [[Layer 4 — Protect|NestShield]] (skip Layers 2–3)**

> "This building has active water risk — [specific finding]. You don't need an estimate or a risk study to know this needs protection now. I can have NestShield monitoring installed within [timeframe]. Want me to walk you through what the system looks like for this building?"

**Use when:** Inspection finds immediate, obvious water risk. No ambiguity. The building needs monitoring yesterday.

---

## The Rule

You never pitch. You show what you found. The next step is obvious because the work made it obvious. If the work doesn't surface a reason to go deeper, don't force it. Invoice, collect, stay in touch.
