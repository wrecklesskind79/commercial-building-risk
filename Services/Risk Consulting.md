# Risk Consulting

**Division:** [[Commercial Building Risk]]
**Funnel Layer:** [[Layer 3 — Advise]]
**Revenue Model:** Project fee or retainer

---

## What It Is

Identify and quantify building risk before it becomes claims, losses, or downtime. You assess exposure, build a plan, and recommend solutions — including technology where it fits.

## Services

- Water risk assessment
- Maintenance planning
- Operational risk review
- Building systems review
- Insurance loss analysis
- Risk mitigation strategies

## Who Buys This

- Owners with recurring losses (especially water)
- Property managers whose premiums are rising
- Insurance companies wanting risk reports
- Lenders requiring risk assessments
- Clients escalated from [[Commercial Inspections]] or [[Preconstruction and Estimating]]

## Deliverable

Risk report. See [[Layer 3 — Advise]] for the full SOP including:
- Risk inventory ranked by likelihood and financial impact
- Root cause analysis for recurring losses
- Maintenance plan recommendations
- Technology recommendations (this is the [[Layer 4 — Protect|Layer 4]] handoff trigger)
- Cost-benefit analysis: prevention vs. next loss
- Insurance premium impact

## How It Feeds the Funnel

Risk assessment → water/freeze exposure needs continuous monitoring → [[NestShield Technology]]
Risk assessment → deferred maintenance needs budgeting → [[Preconstruction and Estimating]]

See [[Handoff Scripts]] for exact transition language.
