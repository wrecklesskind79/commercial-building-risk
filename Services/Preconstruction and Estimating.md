# Preconstruction and Estimating

**Division:** [[Commercial Building Risk]]
**Funnel Layer:** [[Layer 2 — Plan]]
**Revenue Model:** Flat fee or percentage of estimated cost

---

## What It Is

Detailed construction cost estimating and preconstruction support. You take plans (or field measurements) and produce real numbers so owners and contractors can make informed decisions.

## Services

- Quantity takeoffs
- Budget estimates (conceptual through detailed)
- Bid support and bid review
- Scope review and gap analysis
- Capital planning (5-year projections)

## Who Buys This

- Owners who need budgets (often from [[Commercial Inspections]] findings)
- Contractors who need bid support
- Architects who need cost checks
- Property managers planning capital expenditures

## Deliverable

Estimate package. See [[Layer 2 — Plan]] for the full SOP including:
- Cover letter with scope and assumptions
- Summary by division/system
- Detailed line items with quantities and unit costs
- Exclusions and qualifications
- Risk flags (this is the [[Layer 3 — Advise|Layer 3]] handoff trigger)

## How It Feeds the Funnel

Estimate risk flags → recurring costs signal systemic problems → [[Risk Consulting]]
Estimate scope → water/mechanical systems need ongoing protection → [[NestShield Technology]]

See [[Handoff Scripts]] for exact transition language.
