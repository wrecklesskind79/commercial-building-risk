# Commercial Inspections

**Division:** [[Commercial Building Risk]]
**Funnel Layer:** [[Layer 1 — Assess]]
**Revenue Model:** Flat fee per inspection

---

## What It Is

Building condition assessments for commercial properties. You inspect the building, document what you find, and deliver a report that tells the owner exactly what they're dealing with.

## Services

- Building condition assessments
- Property inspections (acquisition due diligence)
- Maintenance observations
- Insurance condition reports

## Who Buys This

- Investors buying a property (due diligence)
- Owners planning maintenance budgets
- Property managers documenting conditions
- Lenders requiring condition reports
- Insurance companies wanting risk assessments

## What You Inspect

- Roof systems
- Building envelope (walls, windows, waterproofing)
- MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing)
- Structural systems
- Interiors
- Site (drainage, parking, paving, landscaping)

## Deliverable

Written report with photos. See [[Layer 1 — Assess]] for the full SOP including:
- Executive summary
- Findings by system (severity-rated)
- Cost opinions (ranges)
- Recommended actions with priority
- Risk exposure summary (this is the [[Layer 2 — Plan|Layer 2]] handoff trigger)

## How It Feeds the Funnel

Inspection findings → deferred maintenance needs budgets → [[Preconstruction and Estimating]]
Inspection findings → ongoing risk exposure → [[Risk Consulting]]
Inspection findings → water/freeze risk → [[NestShield Technology]]

See [[Handoff Scripts]] for exact transition language.
