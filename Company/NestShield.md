# NestShield

**Type:** Parent company
**Website:** https://nestshield.net
**Founder:** [[Stephen Brown]]

---

NestShield is the parent company. Everything operates under this brand. The name comes from the core technology product — 24/7 water and freeze monitoring with automatic shutoff for commercial buildings — but the company is bigger than the product.

## How It Makes Money

Four revenue streams organized as a [[Revenue Funnel]]:

1. [[Commercial Inspections]] — fee per inspection
2. [[Preconstruction and Estimating]] — fee per estimate
3. [[Risk Consulting]] — project fee or retainer
4. [[NestShield Technology]] — install fee + monthly recurring revenue

The first three fund operations. The fourth builds recurring revenue. See [[Revenue Funnel]] for how they connect.
