# Commercial Building Risk

**Type:** Services division under [[NestShield]]
**Website:** https://commercial-building-risk.netlify.app
**Lead:** [[Stephen Brown]]

---

Commercial Building Risk is the services arm of [[NestShield]]. Three service lines, all focused on helping building owners, contractors, investors, and property managers reduce risk before it becomes expensive.

## Service Lines

| Service | Layer | What It Does |
|---|---|---|
| [[Commercial Inspections]] | [[Layer 1 — Assess]] | Know what you're buying or maintaining |
| [[Preconstruction and Estimating]] | [[Layer 2 — Plan]] | Build smarter before construction starts |
| [[Risk Consulting]] | [[Layer 3 — Advise]] | Identify problems before they become claims |

Each service is a standalone revenue generator that also feeds clients toward [[NestShield Technology]] ([[Layer 4 — Protect]]).

## Target Clients

- Building owners
- Contractors
- Investors
- Property managers
- Insurance companies (referral source)
- Lenders (referral source)

## Industries

[[Multifamily]] · [[Hotels]] · [[Office]] · [[Industrial]] · [[Retail]] · [[Healthcare]] · [[Education]]

## Positioning

> "One Building. Multiple Risks. One Trusted Advisor."

The value proposition: buildings don't fit into separate boxes. An inspection finding is also a budgeting question is also a risk exposure is also a monitoring need. CBR connects all four perspectives.
