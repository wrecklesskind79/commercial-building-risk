# Company Structure

```
NestShield (Parent)
├── Commercial Building Risk (Services)
│   ├── Commercial Inspections
│   ├── Preconstruction & Estimating
│   └── Risk Consulting
├── NestShield Technology (Product)
│   ├── Field Operations (install)
│   └── Monitoring & Client Success (recurring)
├── Sales & Business Development (cross-cutting)
└── Future Divisions (see [[Future Divisions]])
```

## How It Works

[[NestShield]] is the parent brand. [[Commercial Building Risk]] is the services division. [[NestShield Technology]] is the product division. Sales serves both.

All divisions share one [[Career Framework]], one [[Competency Matrix]], one [[Promotion Rules]] system. A person can move from inspections to field operations to sales — their [[Career Classes|career class]] stays the same, they just learn a new domain.

## Revenue Flow

See [[Revenue Funnel]]. The services division (CBR) generates project revenue AND feeds qualified leads to the product division (NestShield Technology). The product division generates recurring revenue AND feeds operational data back to the services division.

## Current State

Pre-revenue. [[Stephen Brown]] is the sole operator. The structure exists so that when hiring starts, every role has a home, a class, and a path. See [[Career Framework]].
