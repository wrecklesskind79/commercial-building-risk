Typical observations

Whole building perspective.

  • Roof and drainage from safely accessible areas
  • Exterior walls, windows, doors, grading, and drainage
  • Interior condition and water intrusion indicators
  • Visible plumbing, electrical, and mechanical concerns
  • Deferred maintenance and operational risk indicators
  • Areas requiring qualified specialist evaluation
Clear scope matters.

A commercial inspection is a visual condition assessment of accessible areas. It does not replace engineering, environmental testing, code enforcement, destructive testing, or licensed trade evaluations.
