# Commercial Building Inspections

**Role in funnel:** Entry point. Gets you inside buildings.
**Revenue:** Flat fee per inspection.
**Cert:** CCPIA

## What You Do

Walk the building. Document everything. Deliver an objective report.

No opinions. No recommendations on who to hire or what to fix first. Just findings.

## What You Inspect

- Roof systems
- Building envelope (walls, windows, waterproofing)
- MEP (mechanical, electrical, plumbing)
- Structural systems
- Interiors
- Site (drainage, parking, paving)

## Who Buys

- Investors (acquisition due diligence)
- Owners (maintenance budgets)
- Property managers (documenting conditions)
- Lenders (condition reports)
- Insurance companies (risk assessments)

## Deliverable

Written report with photos:
- Executive summary
- Findings by system (severity-rated)
- Cost ranges (not detailed estimates — that's estimating)
- Risk exposure flags

## Handoff to Consulting

"We found X, Y, Z. The report tells you what's there. If you want my opinion on what to prioritize, how to fix it, and who to call — that's a separate engagement."

The report deliberately withholds expertise. That's the hook.

## Handoff to NestShield

If the inspection finds active water risk or freeze exposure, skip consulting:
"This building needs protection now. NestShield monitors 24/7 and shuts off water before the loss happens."
