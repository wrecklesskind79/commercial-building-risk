# Repair Consulting

**Role in funnel:** The upsell from inspections. Where you sell your 28 years.
**Revenue:** Project fee or retainer.

## What You Do

Give opinions. Tell owners:
- What to fix first
- How to fix it right
- Who to hire (and who to avoid)
- What it should cost (ballpark, not a formal estimate)
- What's going to fail next if they don't act

## Why They Pay

28 years as a builder and plumber. Industrial and residential. You've seen every way a building fails and every way contractors cut corners.

## Who Buys

- Owners who got an inspection report and don't know what to do with it
- Property managers with recurring problems
- Anyone whose insurance premiums are rising
- Owners planning capital improvements

## Deliverable

Consulting report or advisory session:
- Priority list (fix this now, watch this, ignore this)
- Repair approach recommendations
- Contractor guidance
- Timeline and sequencing
- Risk flags that point to NestShield

## Handoff to NestShield

"We can fix the maintenance gaps. But the unknown failures — the pipe at 2 AM, the freeze nobody catches — need continuous monitoring. That's NestShield."

## Pricing

Project fee for one-time advisory. Retainer for ongoing relationship.
