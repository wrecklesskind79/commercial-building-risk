# Construction Estimating

**Role:** Standalone. Funds the operation. Not part of the NestShield funnel.
**Revenue:** Flat fee or percentage of estimated construction cost.

## What You Do

Preconstruction estimating. Take plans or field measurements, produce real numbers. Picking up overflow work from other contractors who need bid support.

## Services

- Quantity takeoffs
- Budget estimates (conceptual through detailed)
- Bid support and bid review
- Scope review and gap analysis

## Who Buys

- Contractors who need overflow bid support
- Owners who need budgets
- Architects who need cost checks
- GCs who are slammed and need takeoff help

## Deliverable

Estimate package:
- Summary by division/system
- Detailed line items with quantities and unit costs
- Exclusions and qualifications
- Cover letter with scope and assumptions

## Pricing

Flat fee or percentage of estimated construction cost. Simple.

## Relationship to NestShield

None required. This makes money independently. If an estimating client happens to need inspections or monitoring, great — but don't force the cross-sell. This pillar exists to fund the operation.
