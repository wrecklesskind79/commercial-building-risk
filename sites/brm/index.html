Frequently Asked Questions

Before you request an inspection.

Who should request a commercial inspection?

Commercial property buyers, owners, investors, and property managers who need documented information about visible building conditions.

Is this an engineering or code-compliance inspection?

No. It is a visual commercial condition inspection of agreed safely accessible areas. Engineering, code enforcement, environmental testing, destructive testing, and licensed trade evaluations are outside the standard scope.

What is the difference between inspection and consulting?

The inspection documents observed conditions under the agreed scope. Consulting is a separate engagement for clients who want help organizing priorities, scopes, or next steps.

Does BRG sell repairs?

No. Inspection findings are not contingent on repair work or contractor sales.
