# NestShield — IoT Water Risk Management

**Website:** nestshield.net
**Role in funnel:** The product. The endgame. Recurring revenue.
**Revenue:** Install fee + monthly monitoring.

## What It Is

Commercial IoT platform. Sensors detect water leaks and freeze conditions. Automatic shutoff valves stop the loss before it starts. 24/7 monitoring.

## Capabilities

- Water leak detection (sensors at high-risk locations)
- Freeze monitoring (attics, exterior walls, unheated spaces)
- Automatic water shutoff (commercial-sized valves)
- Real-time alerts (email, text, app)
- Monitoring dashboard
- Monthly system health reports

## Who Buys

- Owners with water loss history
- Buildings with freeze exposure
- Insurance companies requiring/incentivizing monitoring
- Anyone who's had a pipe burst at 2 AM
- Clients coming from inspections or consulting who have active water risk

## Why It Wins

One prevented event pays for the system. That's the pitch. Show them their loss history, show them the cost of the system, done.

## The Loop

NestShield data feeds back to inspections:
- Sensor data shows degradation → new inspection
- Alerts approaching failure → estimate for replacement
- Patterns across portfolio → new consulting engagement
- Client acquires new building → inspection

The funnel is a loop. NestShield keeps clients in the ecosystem.

## Pricing

One-time install (covers costs + margin). Monthly recurring is the real business.
