# Competency Matrix

Expectations by [[Career Classes|career class]] across 12 competencies. This is what performance reviews measure.

---

| Competency | 1-Trainee | 2-Tech | 3-Specialist | 4-Senior | 5-Manager | 6-Director | 7-Exec |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Technical Knowledge** | Learning | Functional | Proficient | Expert | Expert + teaching | Strategic application | Industry vision |
| **Communication** | Listens, asks | Clear reporting | Client-facing | Presents to execs | Cross-dept coordination | External representation | Board-level |
| **Client Service** | Shadows | Handles routine | Owns relationships | Trusted advisor | Ensures dept satisfaction | Drives retention strategy | Sets service culture |
| **Problem Solving** | Follows procedures | Solves known problems | Solves new problems | Solves systemic problems | Solves org problems | Solves market problems | Solves company problems |
| **Documentation** | Fills templates | Completes standard docs | Creates deliverables | Sets standards | Reviews/approves | Defines requirements | Approves policy |
| **Initiative** | Does what's asked | Anticipates next step | Identifies improvements | Implements improvements | Drives change programs | Sets improvement agenda | Shapes company direction |
| **Safety** | Follows rules | Enforces on self | Enforces on site | Writes procedures | Owns safety culture | Regulatory compliance | Liability/risk ownership |
| **Leadership** | Coachable | Helps peers | Mentors juniors | Leads projects/teams | Manages people | Builds leaders | Sets vision |
| **Training Ability** | N/A | Shows basics | Trains on procedures | Develops programs | Ensures team development | Builds learning culture | Invests in org capability |
| **Business Development** | N/A | Aware of pipeline | Identifies opportunities | Closes or enables deals | Owns dept revenue targets | Owns division growth | Owns company growth |
| **Quality Control** | Follows checklists | Self-checks | Peer reviews | Sets QC standards | Audits dept quality | Drives company QC | Owns brand quality |
| **Time Management** | Meets deadlines when told | Self-manages daily | Manages project timelines | Manages multiple projects | Manages dept capacity | Manages division resources | Manages company priorities |

## How to Use This

- **Hiring:** Match the candidate against the class expectations for the role
- **Reviews:** Rate each competency against the expectations for their current class
- **Promotion:** They should be consistently meeting the NEXT class expectations before they get the title

See [[Promotion Rules]] and [[Career Classes]].
