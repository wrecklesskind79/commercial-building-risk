# Leadership Track

> Advance by building and running teams.

**Path:** Trainee → Technician → Team Lead → Manager → Director → Executive

---

| Level | What Changes | People Responsibility | Financial Accountability | Strategic Role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Trainee | Learning | None | None | None |
| Technician | Doing | Orients new hires | None | None |
| Team Lead | Coordinating | 3–8 direct reports, daily ops | Project budgets | Input on dept goals |
| Manager | Running a department | Full hire/fire/develop | Department budget | Sets dept strategy |
| Director | Running a business line | Builds management team | P&L ownership | Sets division strategy |
| Executive | Running the company | Builds executive team | Company financials | Sets company strategy |

## The Rule

Leadership track people must have done the technical work before leading others in it. No one manages estimators who hasn't estimated. No one manages inspectors who hasn't inspected.
