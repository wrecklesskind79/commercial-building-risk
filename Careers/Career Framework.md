# Career Framework

**Scope:** All divisions under [[NestShield]]
**Owner:** [[Stephen Brown]]

---

Every position in the company maps to one of 7 [[Career Classes]]. Two tracks: [[Technical Track]] (get better at the work) and [[Leadership Track]] (get better at running the business). Both pay equally at equivalent levels. Nobody is forced into management to make more money.

## Quick Reference

| Class | Title | Decision Authority | Experience |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Trainee | None solo | 0–1 yr |
| 2 | Technician / Coordinator | Routine decisions | 1–3 yr |
| 3 | Specialist | Judgment calls on own work | 3–6 yr |
| 4 | Senior Specialist / Project Lead | Full domain authority | 5–10 yr |
| 5 | Manager | Department-level | 7–12 yr |
| 6 | Director | Business line / P&L | 10–15 yr |
| 7 | Executive | Company-wide | 15+ yr |

See [[Career Classes]] for full definitions.

## Tracks

- [[Technical Track]]: Trainee → Technician → Specialist → Senior Specialist → Principal Specialist → Technical Director
- [[Leadership Track]]: Trainee → Technician → Team Lead → Manager → Director → Executive
