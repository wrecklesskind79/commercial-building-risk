# Promotion Rules

Six rules. No exceptions.

---

1. **Nobody skips a class.** Period.
2. **Time-in-role is a guideline, not a guarantee.** Promotion = demonstrated performance at the next level, not years served.
3. **You must be doing the work of the next class before you get the title.** Promotions are recognition, not aspiration.
4. **Track switches are allowed** but require starting at the equivalent class level and demonstrating the new track's competencies. See [[Technical Track]] and [[Leadership Track]].
5. **Every promotion requires:**
   - Documented performance at current level
   - [[Competency Matrix]] met for next level
   - Division-specific certifications complete
   - Recommendation from direct manager
   - Approval one level above
6. **Lateral moves across divisions are encouraged.** The [[Career Classes|class]] stays the same. Technical ramp-up is expected and supported.
