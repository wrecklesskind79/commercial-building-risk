# Deliverable Standards

Every deliverable across all [[Revenue Funnel]] layers must do two things:

1. **Solve the problem the client paid you to solve.** Non-negotiable. This is your reputation.
2. **Clearly surface the next problem they didn't know they had.** This is how the funnel moves.

You never pitch. You show them what you found, and the next step is obvious.

| Layer | Deliverable | Handoff Section |
|---|---|---|

| [[Layer 4 — Protect]] | System documentation | Monitoring data / health reports |

## Rules

- Walk the client through every deliverable. Never just email it.
- Photograph and document everything — over-document.
- Deliver on time or early.
- See [[Documentation Standards]] and [[Handoff Scripts]].
