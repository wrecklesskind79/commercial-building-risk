# Follow-Up Cadence

After every completed engagement across any [[Revenue Funnel]] layer.

---

| When | What |
|---|---|
| Within 1 week | Thank-you call |
| 30 days after delivery | "How's everything going with [the property / project / system]?" |
| 6 months | Check-in, ask about other properties or upcoming projects |
| Annually | "Your building is a year older — anything changing?" |

## Pre-Funnel Contacts

See [[Networking SOP]]:
- Follow up within 24 hours of meeting
- Email, not LinkedIn message
- Reference something specific from the conversation
